Assamese Duck Curry

Assamese Duck Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish from the northeastern state of Assam, India. Tender pieces of duck are simmered in a rich and tangy curry that showcases the unique spices and ingredients of the region. The curry is prepared with a blend of aromatic spices like ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, and dried red chilies, giving it a distinct Assamese flavor. The tender duck meat absorbs the flavors of the spices, resulting in a mouthwatering and satisfying dish. Assamese Duck Curry is often served with steamed rice, allowing you to fully savor the delicious flavors and textures of this traditional Assamese delicacy.

Ingredients:

  • - 1 whole duck, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • - 2 onions, finely chopped
  • - 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • - 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • - 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • - 1 teaspoon mustard oil
  • - 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • - 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • - 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • - 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust according to spice preference)
  • - Salt to taste
  • - Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • - Water for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat mustard oil in a large pan or kadai over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste to the pan and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the duck pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
  5. Reduce the heat to low. Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes to allow the spices to release their flavors.
  6. Add enough water to cover the duck pieces. Bring the curry to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pan with a lid and let it simmer for about 45-60 minutes, or until the duck is tender and cooked through. Stir occasionally and add more water if needed.
  7. Once the duck is cooked, adjust the seasoning according to taste. Garnish the Assamese Duck Curry with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or traditional Assamese bread like "pitha" or "paratha".

Assamese Duck Curry is a delightful and flavorsome dish that captures the essence of Assamese cuisine. The combination of aromatic spices, tender duck meat, and the richness of mustard oil creates a delicious and comforting curry. Enjoy this traditional Assamese delicacy with your loved ones!
