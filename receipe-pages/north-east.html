Assamese Duck Curry

Assamese Duck Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish from the northeastern state of Assam, India. Tender pieces of duck are simmered in a rich and tangy curry that showcases the unique spices and ingredients of the region. The curry is prepared with a blend of aromatic spices like ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, and dried red chilies, giving it a distinct Assamese flavor. The tender duck meat absorbs the flavors of the spices, resulting in a mouthwatering and satisfying dish. Assamese Duck Curry is often served with steamed rice, allowing you to fully savor the delicious flavors and textures of this traditional Assamese delicacy.

Jadoh

Jadoh is a traditional rice dish from the northeastern state of Meghalaya, India. It is a flavorful and hearty preparation that combines rice with meat, usually pork, along with a blend of aromatic spices. The rice is cooked with the meat, onions, ginger, garlic, and a variety of local spices, resulting in a dish that is rich in flavors and textures. The meat imparts its savory essence to the rice, creating a delightful combination. Jadoh is known for its comforting taste and is often enjoyed as a main course or during festive occasions. It is a beloved dish in Meghalaya, showcasing the culinary heritage and unique flavors of the region.
