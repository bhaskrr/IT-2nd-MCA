Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish that consists of grilled or roasted chicken tikka (marinated chicken) cooked in a creamy and flavorful tomato-based sauce. It is a rich and aromatic dish that is enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients:

For Chicken Tikka

  • - 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • - 1 cup plain yogurt
  • - 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • - 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • - 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • - 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • - 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • - 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • - 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • - 1 tablespoon oil
  • - Salt to taste
  • - Skewers for grilling

For Tikka Masala Sauce

  • - 2 tablespoons butter
  • - 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • - 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • - 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • - 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • - 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • - 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • - 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • - 1 cup tomato puree
  • - 1 cup heavy cream
  • - 1 tablespoon dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • - Salt to taste
  • - Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, oil, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well with the marinade. Let the chicken marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight in the refrigerator for best results.
  2. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and grill or roast them until they are cooked through and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. In a large pan or skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste to the pan and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Stir to combine the spices with the onions.
  6. Add tomato puree to the pan and cook for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oil separates from the mixture.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and add heavy cream to the pan. Stir well to combine the cream with the tomato gravy. Simmer for a few minutes.
  8. Crush the dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) between your palms and add them to the sauce. Stir well.
  9. Season with salt to taste. Adjust the consistency of the sauce by adding water if needed.
  10. Add the grilled chicken tikka pieces to the sauce and simmer for 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
`

Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice. It is a flavorful and satisfying dish that is loved by many. Enjoy preparing and sharing this delicious Chicken Tikka Masala recipe!
